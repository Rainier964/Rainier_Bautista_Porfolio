About Me

I am Rainier Bautista, born on September 6, 2004, and I am currently 20 years old. I live in Dasmariñas, Cavite, and I am a first-year college student at the National College of Science and Technology (NCST), pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering.

My dream is to become a software engineer, with the goal of achieving success both domestically and globally. I aspire to develop innovative applications and systems that enhance everyday life by making tasks more efficient, improving communication, and providing seamless access to information. One of my biggest ambitions is to create a software solution that can help people in their daily lives, whether through automation, convenience, or innovation. Additionally, I have always dreamed of developing my own project—an application or platform that truly makes a positive impact on the world.

Beyond academics, I enjoy playing sports such as badminton and volleyball. I also love spending time with my friends and classmates, whether celebrating birthdays or simply hanging out and making memories together.
